All About ATM

What is ATM?

ATM stands for Applied Technology Magnet and is a 3 year computer program at Brancroft Middle School. In ATM, students are introduced to different programs on the computer and get to learn and explore them.

In 6th grade, I was introduced to the basics of computer programs, learned how to type, and how to be safe on the web.

In 7th grade, I learned graphics and computer aided drafting.

In 8th grade, I learned about video and music creation and coding.
